Lasagna

A simply delicious dish

This is the only thing my niece can make, not joking. I believe she first got the idea for eating lasagna over sphagetti some time in highschool. It's one of the only things she can cook ever since.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • parmesan cheese
  • ground beef

Steps

  1. With enough water to cover pasta, boil water to cook pasta al dente
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef, break apart and cook thoroughly over medium high heat
  3. Add tomato sauce and cooked pasta to ground beef skillet and mix together
  4. Serve on a plate and grate parmesan cheese over pasta

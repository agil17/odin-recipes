Garbanzo Salad

Stop Thinking So Much!

When you're in and out and on go, a meal prepped garbanzo salad for the week is just what you need, don't think about it, just grab it and go.

Ingredients

  • garbando
  • tomato
  • jalapeno
  • onion
  • bell pepper

Steps

  1. Cook your garbanzo, if canned, rinse and dry garbanzo beans with napkins
  2. dice your vegetables
  3. mix garbanzo and diced veggies

I like to eat this at home already prepped out, I'll just add mayo, seasoning, and a little bit of lemon juice. I'd eat it along with saltine crackers.
